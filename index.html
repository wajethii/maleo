About Our School

Discover the Maleo difference

Established in 2005, Maleo Preparatory has been at the forefront of providing quality education in Nairobi for over 18 years. Our mission is to cultivate intellectual curiosity, foster creativity, and develop responsible global citizens.

Our campus, located in the heart of Nairobi, offers state-of-the-art facilities including modern classrooms, science laboratories, a library, sports fields, and dedicated art and music studios.
